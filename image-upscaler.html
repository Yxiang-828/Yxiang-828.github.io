üîß Technical Details

Real-ESRGAN (GPU-Accelerated GAN)

Best for: Major quality improvements, blurry/low-quality images
Technology: Generative Adversarial Networks trained on millions of images
Speed: 2-5 seconds per image (GPU)
VRAM Usage: ~1-4GB depending on image size

EDSR (CPU-Based CNN)

Best for: Precise sharpening, near-complete images
Technology: Deep Convolutional Neural Networks with TensorFlow
Speed: 15-30 seconds per image (CPU)
Note: More conservative - better for subtle improvements
